Moroccan Tagine

Description of the dish

Moroccan Vegetable Tagine is a delightful and aromatic dish that captures the essence of Moroccan cuisine. This vegetarian tagine is a slow-cooked stew that combines an array of colorful and flavorful vegetables, creating a symphony of tastes and textures.

Ingredients

    CHICKEN

    • 1 3/4 lbs chicken thighs, skin removed and fat trimmed (OR chicken breasts for reduced fat)
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 small red onion, diced
    • 1 tablespoon The Fit Cook Everyday Blend
    • 1 tablespoon turmeric
    • 2 teaspoons cumin
    • 1 teaspoon ginger powder
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped parsley

    VEGGIES

    • 2 red potato, peeled and chopped
    • 2 medium zucchini, sliced into long chunks
    • 1/2 cup pitted olives

STEPS

  1. Place ALL of the ingredients for the chicken
  2. Place the tagine on medium high heat and cook uncovered for about 10 minutes
  3. After 10 minutes, begin to place the VEGGIES on top of the chicken, carefully forming a pyramid.
  4. Top it off with cilantro and parsley and a generous pinch of sea salt & pepper
  5. Cover the tagine (or Dutch oven pot) and place on low-medium heat. Cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the veggies are cooked through to crisp-tenderness
