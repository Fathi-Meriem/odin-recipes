Moroccan Tagine

Description of the dish

Moroccan Vegetable Tagine is a delightful and aromatic dish that captures the essence of Moroccan cuisine. This vegetarian tagine is a slow-cooked stew that combines an array of colorful and flavorful vegetables, creating a symphony of tastes and textures.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of fish (whiting in half-fillet, hake cut into slices, sardines in fillet, sea bream in fillet, anchovies)
  • 250 g of durum wheat flour
  • salt

STEPS

  1. Dip the pieces of fish in the charmoula.
  2. Leave to marinate in the refrigerator for 1 hour
  3. Heat the oil in a frying pan and reduce the heat to medium temperature.
  4. Flour the pieces of fish and fry them in hot oil.
